About Albert Academy

Albert Academy in Hossana, Ethiopia is a community-focused private school offering quality education from Kindergarten to Grade 8. We are committed to nurturing well-rounded students through strong academics, creativity, and values that prepare them for the future.

Our Mission

To educate, empower, and inspire students to become confident learners, creative thinkers, and responsible citizens who will lead with integrity and vision.

Academic Program

A balanced curriculum in maths, science, languages, and social studies with qualified teachers and active learning methods.

Beyond the Classroom

Sports, clubs, creative projects, and community service that build teamwork, confidence, and real-world skills.

Parent Partnership

Through our digital platform, parents can track progress, receive updates, and communicate directly with teachers.

At Albert Academy, we don’t just teach lessons—we shape lives. Our students grow into Talented Students, Future Leaders, Creative Minds, and Active Learners, ready to shine in every step of life.
